ability - a unit of action perfomed by a card.
card - a card as it appears in a pile. it can only ever be in a pile. it will be in the stack pile but won't be the object on the stack
destroy - when a pawn is destroyed it is moved to it's owners graveyard
pawn - a pawn is the on board representation of a card
pile - a collection of cards in a game of twofer
the stack - where abilities go after they are cast and before they resolve.
